High Conductivity Copper Busbars: Powering Nitro Metal's Excellence

Nitro Metal introduces an advanced range of copper busbars, engineered to meet the most demanding electrical distribution and transmission requirements. Our copper busbars are designed for superior conductivity, durability, and efficiency, making them ideal for a wide range of industrial, commercial, and utility applications. With a commitment to quality and innovation, Nitro Metal’s copper busbars ensure optimal performance, reliability, and safety for your electrical systems.

Key Features

Explore the outstanding features of our Copper Busbar

  • High Electrical Conductivity:

    Ensures efficient power distribution with minimal power loss, making them ideal for high-current applications.

  • Excellent Thermal Conductivity:

    Facilitates effective heat dissipation, which is critical for maintaining operational stability and extending the lifespan of electrical systems.

  • Superior Corrosion Resistance:

    Treated surfaces resist oxidation and corrosion, ensuring long-term reliability and performance in harsh environments.

  • Customizable Dimensions:

    Available in various sizes and thicknesses to meet specific installation requirements, ensuring flexibility in design and application.

  • Easy Installation:

    Designed for straightforward integration into existing electrical systems, reducing installation time and costs.

Copper Busbar Specifications:

Specification Value
Material High Conductivity Copper (Cu-ETP)
Conductivity ≥ 99.90% IACS
Tensile Strength 200 - 250 MPa
Electrical Resistivity 1.7241 μΩ.cm at 20°C
Thickness 5mm - 15mm
Width 10mm - 200mm
Length Up to 6000mm

Copper Busbar Mechanical Specifications:

Property Value
Density 8.93 g/cm³
Melting Point 1083°C
Elastic Modulus 117 GPa
Thermal Expansion 16.5 μm/m°C
Thermal Conductivity 390 W/(m·K)

Types of Copper Busbars Available

  • Flat Copper Busbars:

    Ideal for compact installations, offering efficient power distribution and heat dissipation.

  • Edged Copper Busbars:

    Designed with enhanced mechanical strength for support in heavy-duty applications.

  • Flexible Copper Busbars:

    Suitable for applications requiring high flexibility and vibration resistance.

  • Insulated Copper Busbars:

    Coated with an insulating material, these are used where additional safety and protection against electrical shocks are necessary.

Applications & Uses of Copper Busbars

  • Electrical switchboards and panel boards
  • Power transformers and substations
  • Bus ducts and trunking systems
  • Renewable energy systems (solar, wind)
  • Electric vehicles (EV) charging stations
  • Industrial machinery and automation systems
